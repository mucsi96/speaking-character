# CLAUDE.md

## Conventions

- **Always use English filenames**, even when the file's content is in another
  language. The Schatzsuche content is German/Hungarian, but files are named in
  English (e.g. the C1 print cards are `00-instructions.svg`, `01-task.svg`, not
  `00-anleitung.svg`/`01-aufgabe.svg`). Localized text stays inside the file.
